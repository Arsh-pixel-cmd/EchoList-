Overview

EchoList is a local-first task manager. Your data stays on your device. We do not operate servers, collect personal information, or track your activity.

What we do not collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone)
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device fingerprints or ad IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos, contacts, or files

Sonic Sync (Peer-to-Peer)

Sonic Sync connects two devices using ultrasonic audio and a direct WebRTC connection.

Direct: Data goes device-to-device, never through a server.
Ephemeral: Nothing is stored after the session ends.
Local only: Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi network.

Third-party services

The web version uses Vercel Analytics for anonymous, aggregate page-view statistics only. No personal data or task content is included. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party SDKs, trackers, or ad networks are included.

Data storage

All data is stored locally on your device ‚Äî browser localStorage on web, and Capacitor local storage on Android/iOS (sandboxed to the app).

Uninstalling the app or clearing browser data will permanently delete all tasks and notification history.

Children's privacy

EchoList does not collect data from any user, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.
